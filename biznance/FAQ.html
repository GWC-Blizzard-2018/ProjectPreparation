To ask us a question, find our email on the Contact page

How did you get your information?

We got our information by interviewing various college students as well as students who have graduated recently. We also researched various online resources, as shown in our Sources below.

How can I figure out what major I want to go in?

There are various sites online where one can take a quiz to figure out the best majors for them. Of course, they’re not always completely accurate but they give a good idea of where to start. Here are some links: It’s not the end of the world going into college without knowing your major, either. Many people end up discovering/changing their major halfway through!

Where can I find additional college resources?

Feel free to look at our Sources below to see some of the resources we used to make this website. Other than that, collegeboard has a myriad of helpful information. Google is also a great resource for any further questions you may have.
