About Us

Who we are

Welcome to College Prep For Teens, your number one source for all things college related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best information, with a focus on preparation for High school students. Project Preparation was founded in 2018 by Aspen, Grace, Hailey, and Samantha. The project has come a long way from its beginnings as just a mere concept. When we first started out, it was solely driven by a passion for helping other students gain knowledge about all the concepts of college. We aim to help any and all students who are interested in furthering their education beyond high school. We hope you find useful information on our website, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
