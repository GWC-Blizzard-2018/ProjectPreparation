In-State VS Out-of-State Colleges

In-State Schools

  • Easier to get in to
  • Cheaper tuition
  • Can stay at home
  • Know people
  • Familiar with area
  • Not As intimidating
  • Can visit home more often

Out-of-State Schools

  • Can help you gain confidence
  • Allows you to experience new life styles
  • Hard to get in to some colleges
  • Can be costly
